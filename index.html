Welcome!

I'm Hana (Hannaneh Barahouei Pasandi), and welcome to my personal website! I'm a CS PhD student broadly interested in different aspects of systems & networking, machine learning, and algorithms. My recent focus is on applied machine learning to improve IEEE 802.11 WLANs design in MAC and PHY layers. Prior to my PhD program, I worked as research assistant in LIG lab in Grenoble, France and Columbia University in New York City, NY.

For more information on my previous and ongoing projects please visit Projects page.
